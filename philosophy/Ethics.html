4. Ethics

Ethics (or Moral) is the part of Philosophy that studies the nature of Right and Wrong. Ethics seeks to define concepts such as:. Good and Evil Right and Wrong Justice and Crime What is Ethics? What are true values? What is Moral? What moral principles govern a person's behaviour or activities? Meta Ethics Where do ethical principles come from? What do ethical principles mean? Are ethical principles social inventions? Are ethical principles created from emotions? Normative Ethics Normative Ethics involves the study of common moral standards. Always do to others what others to do to you.
