5. Aesthetics

Aesthetics (or Esthetics) is the part of Philosophy that studies the nature of Art, Beauty, and Taste. What is Art? What makes Art good? What makes Art bad? What is a work of Art? What is the purpose of Art?. What is Beauty? What makes a woman beautiful? Qualities that pleases the aesthetic senses, especially the sight: Attractive Beautiful Cute Gorgeous Handsome Lovely Pretty What is Taste? Philosophy tries to explore all the ways taste can be defined. Taste can be objective: Sweet Salt Sour Bitter Savory Taste can be subjective: "What a beatiful island" "I like tall women" "I love classic music" "The beauty of poetry.
