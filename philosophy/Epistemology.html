2. Epistemology

Epistemology is the part of Philosophy that studies the nature of Knowledge. (Greek Epistēmē = Knowledge) What is Knowledge? Sensing is our primary tools to gain knowledge. Can we also use Intelligent Reason? Can we use Belief? Senses Seeing Hearing Smelling Tasting Feeling We can See the colors of nature, we can Hear the birds, we can Smell the flowers, we can Taste the fruit, we can Feel the heat of the sun. Intelligent Reasoning Man can also understand abstract knowledge: E = mc2 The earth is round It is wrong to kill How do we gain abstract knowledge? Belief Most people believe that a God exists, even if it cannot be proven by reason. Some believe that every man is created by God. Some believe there are Angels. Some people will need no justification. Faith is enough. Some people will justfy their Faith by referring to episodes where God has spoken directly to living persons. Mysticism The theory that it is possible to understand reality without intelligent reason is called mysticism. Is there an other reality in addition to the one that we can see and sense? Is it possible to gain knowledge about this other reality by using other forms of cognition than senses and intelligent reason?

現実は、記憶の合成で出来ている。
　 記憶の種類は６種類ある。
　 ・視覚
　 ・聴覚
　 ・触覚
　 ・嗅覚
　 ・味覚
　 ・言語

　 過去も未来も現在も、記憶の合成でできている。
